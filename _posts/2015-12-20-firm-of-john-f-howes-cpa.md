# About

Whether it is helping you manage your business or helping you through the tax maze, you get the advantage of my extensive experience and education.
![](urn://i2.wp.com/www.cpajohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/keyboard2.jpg?resize=204%2C117)

In-depth experience in corporate, partnership and individual taxation. Employed by two CPA firms as a tax manager and a manager-owner in two other CPA firms. Also CEO/President of a retail store chain, an internal audit manager for a major financial institution, lectured and taught continuing education and college courses on various corporate and tax subjects.

**Founder/Principal**  
Firm of  [John F Howes CPA][55]  
Vancouver, WA  
Certified Public Accounting firm with emphasis on individual, corporate and partnership tax compliance and consulting.

**President/CEO**  
RTO Inc.  
Clearwater, FL  
Directed, managed and supervised all aspects of a two store retail chain of prerecorded music stores for 11 years. Activities included responsibility for all data processing operations, internet site development, accounting, and tax compliance functions. Also assisted with marketing, sales and purchasing. Successfully negotiated asset acquisitions of three other music retailers. Over a period of five years, accomplished a more than five-fold increase in gross revenue. Successfully installed a point of sale system replacing a manual system. Also managed a tax and accounting practice within one of the chain stores.

**Partner**  
Smith, Howes and Company, CPAs  
Tampa, Florida  
Founded and managed a tax and accounting practice in Tampa, Florida for 8 years. Practice emphasis included tax and financial accounting, business consulting and tax compliance for C Corporations, S Corporations, Partnerships, Sole Proprietors (self-employed individuals) and Personal Income Tax preparation. Supervised accounting and computer operations. Practice also included forensic accounting engagements. Practice was purchased and merged into another CPA firm. Served as [National Association of Securities Dealers][56] (NASD) Arbitrator in resolving actions brought by investors against stockbrokers and brokerage firms. Also was partner-in-charge of all audit and review engagements.

**Manager of Taxation**  
Tampa, Florida  
Employed with two Tampa Bay area accounting firms, Harper, Van Scoik & Company, CPAs, PA and Flemings & Frey, CPAs, PA. Engaged in extensive tax research at the corporate, trust and partnership levels. Provided tax research assistance to audit, bookkeeping and tax compliance personnel within the organization. Analyzed tax consequences of major transactions, represented clients before Federal Tax Authorities for both routine and audit activities, compliance with all Federal and State income, Information, Sales Tax, Tangible/Intangible tax filings for various high income individuals, closely held corporations, general and limited partnerships.

**Manager of Internal Audit**  
Eastern Financial Florida Credit Union  
Miami, Florida  
Responsible for all internal audit functions of a major financial institution. Reported directly to the President, the Board of Directors and the Supervisory Committee. Acted as liaison for outside auditors.

Certified Consultant -- Microsoft Office Accounting****Authorized IRS E-file Provider  
IRS Audit Representation  
Member -- Washington State Tax Consultants**
